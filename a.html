Privacy Policy for Master Quantitative Aptitude

Last updated: June 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Master Quantitative Aptitude (“we”, “us” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address or username if you sign up.
  • User Content: Any quizzes, notes, or feedback you submit through the App.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers.

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App’s features and content.
  • To personalize your learning experience (e.g. recommend practice sets).

  • To communicate important notices (e.g. updates, security alerts).

4. Sharing of Your Information

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We share data with Analytics and Crash-Reporting providers under strict confidentiality.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us to have it deleted.

8. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of analytics tracking by following instructions in your device settings.
  • You may request access to, update, or delete your personal data by emailing us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy here with an updated “Last updated” date.
